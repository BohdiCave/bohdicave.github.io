Welcome!

I am a full-stack web developer located in Philadelphia, PA. My primary interest is in providing my expertise and services to individuals, organizations, or businesses operating in several fields of interest:

  1. Environmental protection: nature conservation, ecosystem maintenance, addressing the consequences of climate change.
  2. Artistic and cultural pursuits: creative and cultural initatives, projects focused on cross-cultural dialogue, teaching tolerance, and developing cultural compentence.
  3. Scientific and educational initiatives: particularly those intersecting with art, ecology, and wider social issues.
